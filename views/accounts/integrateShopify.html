Connect to your Shopify Seller Account

With ezyCommerce, you can sell on shopify and use ezyCommerce as the fulfillment solution for your shopify orders. In order to do this, you need to add your shopify seller account on the ezyCommerce platform.

    We can synchronize the following information with shopify –
  • - Fulfillment Orders - Once you are connected, we will start polling shopify servers periodically to check for new orders under your account. All new and modified orders will be downloaded into your ezyCommerce account.
  • - Order Status – When an order is shipped, status and tracking information is pushed to shopify.
    Follow the procedure below to add your shopify account to ezyCommerce.
  1. Click ‘Connect to shopify’, you’ll be directed to the shopify.com website. Simply enter your shopify credentials and it will link up. shopify has unified logins between all their individual websites for different countries.
  2. Enter the required account information – your username and password. Shopify uses oAuth security authentication mechanism hence nobody including us will not have access to your account password.
  3. After logging in, select “I agree” to grant access for ezyCommerce to access your shopify account and you’ll be redirected back to the ezyCommerce Connections page.
  4. You need to make sure that your shopify listings have the same Merchant SKU in the ‘SKU’ field so that when we import an order from shopify, we can identify the relevant products to ship. Other forms of product identification such as Product ID, ezy-SKU will not work. Only products with matching ‘Merchant SKU’ will be identified. If you are selling multiple variants of an item, click here to know the process to setup variant SKUs in your shopify listing.
  5. If you would like to update shipment tracking information back to shopify, check “Update Order Tracking Status” in the Connections page. Once your orders have shipped, we will automatically push tracking information into shopify and change the order status to “Shipped”.
Note: At this time, we support only Direct shopify connection. We do not support shopify via Paypal IPN connection.
