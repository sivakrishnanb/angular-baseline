Connect to your amazon Seller Account

With ezyCommerce, you can sell on amazon and use ezyCommerce as the fulfillment solution for your amazon orders. In order to do this, you need to add your amazon seller account on the ezyCommerce platform.

    We can synchronize the following information with amazon –
  • - Fulfillment Orders - Once you are connected, we will start polling amazon servers periodically to check for new orders under your account. All new and modified orders will be downloaded into your ezyCommerce account.
  • - Order Status – When an order is shipped, status and tracking information is pushed to amazon.
  • - Product mapping – You need to make sure that your amazon listings have the same Merchant SKU in the ‘SKU’ field so that when we import an order from amazon, we can identify the relevant products to ship. Other forms of product identification such as Product ID, ezy-SKU will not work. Only products with matching ‘Merchant SKU’ will be identified. If you are selling multiple variants of an item.

    What all you need to add amazon channel.
  1. Channel Name - a name that must be unique across all the channels you hold in ezycommerce.
  2. Channel Country - The country that you hold amazon channel account, if you want to add different countries, different channel name with the specific country name has to be added.
  3. Store Email - an email id that you have used to login amazon seller account, and its a reference in our ezyCommerce.
  4. Seller ID - a unique identifier which was generated during amazon account creation and this will help to access your amazon seller account.
  5. Marketplace ID - a unique identifier which was generated during amazon account creation and this will help to access your amazon seller account.
  6. Marketplace Auth Token - a token which was generated during amazon account creation and it helps to give access to your amazon account credentials.
  7. Merchant Token - a token or identifier which must be unique across all your amazon channel accounts.
