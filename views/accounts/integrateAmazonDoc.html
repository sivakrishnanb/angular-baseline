Adding Amazon as a channel

    In order to add Amazon as a channel, follow these steps:
  1. Log in to your ezyCommerce account.
  2. Click on My Account > Connections.
  3. Scroll to the bottom of the page where all market places are listed.
  4. You will notice button to add Amazon channel.

  5. Be sure to click on ‘connect’ under an icon applicable.
  6. Once you click the button a form will load, which will look like this:


  7. The information required in this form will be available on Amazon Marketplace Web Service or Amazon MWS site. Follow the below instructions to fill the form.
    1. Visit the Amazon Marketplace Web Service (MWS).
    2. Click on ‘Sign Up for MWS’ on the Amazon MWS page

    3. Login with your Amazon seller account ID and password
    4. Select 'I want to give a developer access to my Amazon seller account with MWS.' option
    5. Mention ezycommerce as Developer’s Name and use our Developer Account Number : 4044-9725-0388 and then click ‘Next’


    6. On the next page tick all check boxes and click 'Next'


    7. The next page will display your generated ‘Seller ID’ , ‘Marketplace ID’ and 'Marketplace MWS Auth Token'


  8. Copy this ‘Seller ID’ , ‘Marketplace ID’ and and 'Marketplace MWS Auth Token' paste it in the form you saw in step 6.


  9. Click ‘Add channel’ and your Amazon channel is ready to use.
