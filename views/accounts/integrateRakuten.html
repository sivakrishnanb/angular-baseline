Connect to your rakuten Seller Account

With ezyCommerce, you can sell on rakuten and use ezyCommerce as the fulfillment solution for your rakuten orders. In order to do this, you need to add your rakuten seller account on the ezyCommerce platform.

    We can synchronize the following information with rakuten –
  • - Fulfillment Orders - Once you are connected, we will start polling rakuten servers periodically to check for new orders under your account. All new and modified orders will be downloaded into your ezyCommerce account.
  • - Order Status – When an order is shipped, status and tracking information is pushed to rakuten.
Product mapping – You need to make sure that your rakuten listings have the same Merchant SKU in the ‘SKU’ field so that when we import an order from rakuten, we can identify the relevant products to ship. Other forms of product identification such as Product ID, ezy-SKU will not work. Only products with matching ‘Merchant SKU’ will be identified. If you are selling multiple variants of an item.
    What all you need to add rakuten channel.
  1. ezyCommerce Channel Name - a name that must be unique across all the channels you hold in ezycommerce.
  2. Email - an email id that you have used in rakuten seller account, and its a reference in our ezyCommerce.
  3. Marketplace ID - Marketplace Identifier must be selected as a specific country (eg for a Singapore, UK, Germany).
  4. Shop URL - The Shop URL is the unique name of your Rakuten Store (you do not need to enter the whole URL here, just the part with your unique store name - e.g. MyAmazingStore from http://www.rakuten.co.uk/shop/MyAmazingStore). For more info contact your Rakuten Account Manager.
  5. API Key - This is a code that is required to verify that communication between ezyCommerce and Rakuten are authentic. If you have not been provided with an API Key then contact your Rakuten Account Manager and they will be able to provide it.
Note : if your store URL is http://www.rakuten.co.uk/shop/MyAmazingStore, enter only MyAmazingStore
